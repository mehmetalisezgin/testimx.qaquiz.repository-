Q 3 of 10

Which of the following is NOT a valid assertion in TestimX?

Foundations

Assertions

Why this is correct

While most testing frameworks provide an `isNotEqual` or similar assertion, TestimX uses a different convention for negativity.

  • It emphasizes direct assertions.
  • Negations are handled using `Assert.isFalse()`.

For example, to check for inequality:

Assert.isFalse(value1 === value2);
